CuePad Help

Quick start + troubleshooting (mobile & desktop).
Open CuePad

Quick Start (30 seconds)

  1. Open CuePad: Launch CuePad
  2. Tap “Tap to Arm Audio”
  3. Tap Import Sounds and choose audio files
  4. Drag a sound from Library onto a pad
  5. Tap pads to play

Hotkeys on desktop: 1–9, 0, Q, W, E, R, T, Y

Adding Sounds

  • Mobile: use Import Sounds
  • Desktop: you can also drag audio files into the Library drop zone
  • Supported: WAV, MP3, OGG, M4A/AAC (varies by device)

iPhone Tips

  • No sound? Make sure your iPhone is not on Silent Mode.
  • Always tap “Tap to Arm Audio” once before playing.
  • If files are greyed out, pick files stored On My iPhone (not cloud-only).

Streaming / OBS

  • Easiest: play CuePad on your computer and capture desktop audio.
  • Phone as soundboard: you may need an audio route (Bluetooth, cable, interface) depending on your setup.
  • CuePad is designed to be fast and simple; your capture method determines “stream routing.”

Troubleshooting

  • Nothing plays: tap Arm Audio and turn volume up.
  • iPhone silent: flip the silent switch OFF.
  • Import works but pads empty: drag a Library sound onto a pad.
  • Old version showing: refresh / reopen (PWAs can cache).
